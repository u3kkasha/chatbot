# Safety & Source Control Constraints

## The Animate Beings Ban

- **CRITICAL:** Emojis, icons, or images of animate beings (people, animals, bugs, robots, etc.) are strictly prohibited in the codebase, commits, or documentation.
- Use only abstract or inanimate objects (e.g. 🔧, ⚙️, ⚡, 📝, 📦, 🔒).
- Prohibited examples: 👤, 🤖, 🚀, 🐛, 🐳, 🤝.

## Source Control Rules

- Format: Conventional commits in the `type(scope): message` format.
- Enforce gitmojis in commit messages (using abstract/inanimate gitmojis only).
- Do not rewrite files completely; use surgical edits where possible.
